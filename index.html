About our shop

A neighborhood thrift shop built around reuse, style, and joyful discovery.

We believe secondhand shopping should feel inspiring, not overwhelming. That's why our store is thoughtfully arranged, regularly refreshed, and filled with pieces chosen for quality, usefulness, and charm. Whether you're searching for a vintage jacket, a well-made chair, or a small home detail with soul, we make the hunt worth it.
